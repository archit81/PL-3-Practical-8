List Of Top Best 4 Most Expected Teams Of IPL 2020

The 13th edition of the Indian Premier League is a few months away, So which are the most expected teams in the upcoming IPL 2020?

Chennai Super Kings – The Most Consistent IPL 2020 Team

Well, needless to say about CSK’s consistent performance from the past IPL seasons from 2008 to 2019. They are 3 times title winners(2010,2011 and 2018), 8 times Final Qualifier (2008,2010,2011,2012,2013,2015,2018 and 2019) and All-time playoff qualifier.

Mumbai Indians – The Strongest IPL 2020 Team

Mumbai Indians, One of the strongest teams in IPL won 4 titles out of 5 IPL finals played. Mumbai Indians won four times in 2013, 2015, 2017, and 2019.

Delhi Capitals IPL 2020 Team

Many of you would be surprised to see the DC in this list as Delhi never even qualified for the IPL final in any of the previous season. But the, in the last IPL season, the young squad of DC was rocking from the league stage to the eliminator round.

Kolkata Knight Riders IPL Team

The KKR team have won the IPL titles 2 times, in IPL 2012 and in IPL 2014. This is one of the most expected teams in the IPL due to its current buys in the IPL auction.

IPL 2020: BCCI adopts this new way to stop betting

The BCCI has tied up with UK-based company SportRadar to provide services through its Fraud Detection System (FDS) to prevent betting and other corrupt activities during the upcoming IPL.

BCCI signed an agreement with SportRadar

An IPL source told news agency PTI, ‘Yes, the BCCI has tied up with Sportradar for this year’s IPL. They will work closely with ACU and provide their services. ‘ He said, “The Sportsperson had recently put half a dozen matches of the Goa Football League under suspicion.

FDS will detect betting related to betting

The BCCI ACU recently explored different specimens of betting during the state-level T20 league, including the Tamil Nadu Premier League (TNPL). A major betting company stopped placing bets due to different bets being placed.According to the SportRadar, the Fraud Detection System (FDS) is a specialized service that detects betting related to sports betting.
